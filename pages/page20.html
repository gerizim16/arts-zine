As a manga about sports, Haikyu!! also inevitably tackles health. When Hinata’s team loses their most important match yet after Hinata collapses from fever midway through, Hinata is forced to learn a lesson he’ll carry for the rest of his life: Happiness and success are useless if you don’t take care of yourself. Years into the future, he remains conscious of eating unhealthy food or overstraining himself. Many Olympic-level players of the series are the same, hammering down an oft-overlooked truth— success doesn’t need to come at the expense of your health. In fact, it’s the opposite: You’ll perform your best when you’re not overtired and weak.

Furudate manages to tackle all these potentially-sad themes in a way that feels victorious and constructive, as characters eventually get through their conflict. All these themes are often not just one-time things: Haikyu!! circles back on them often, and it hits harder each time.
