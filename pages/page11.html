Bliss: The Default Windows XP Wallpaper
The most-viewed photograph of all time?

If you were born before the year 2005, and have used a computer around that time, then chances are you may have seen this photo.

A lower quality version of "Bliss". Microsoft owns all rights to the original image. (Source: Atlas Obscura)

Estimated by some to have been seen on a billion computers globally, the default Windows XP wallpaper is said to be the most viewed digital image in history. Officially named "Bliss", the origin of this photo has mystified many people, some having suggested different locations as the actual site, and even others have speculated that the image was either not real, a combination of separate images, or a heavily modified photograph. Despite these theories, this image is a virtually unedited photograph of a hill and the sky taken in California.
